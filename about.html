About the Student Feedback Portal

The Student Feedback Portal is an online platform developed to strengthen communication between students and educators at Sunway University. It provides a secure, efficient, and transparent way for students to share their feedback on courses, lecturers, and campus facilities.

By collecting constructive feedback, the portal aims to enhance academic quality, improve the learning experience, and support Sunway’s mission of continuous improvement in higher education.

Our Objectives

  • Promote open and effective communication between students and staff.
  • Encourage data-driven improvement in teaching and course design.
  • Ensure feedback is reviewed with fairness and confidentiality.

How It Works

  1. Students log in using their university credentials.
  2. They select the course, lecturer, or facility they want to review.
  3. Feedback is analyzed and used to enhance the learning environment.

Why It Matters

Student voices play a crucial role in shaping a better educational experience. This portal empowers students to share insights that help improve academic content, teaching quality, and campus life.
